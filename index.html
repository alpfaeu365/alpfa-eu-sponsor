About ALPFA EU

A chapter built on community, ambition, and opportunity.

Since our establishment in Spring 2025, ALPFA Elmhurst University has grown into a dynamic community for students of diverse backgrounds at EU. Grounded in three pillars — professional development, philanthropy and volunteering, and social connection — we create meaningful experiences that prepare students for the corporate world.

Our programming spans firm office tours, industry panels, career workshops, cross-organizational collaborations, and community service. In our first year we have partnered with RSM, hosted an AI career workshop, collaborated with the Public Health Club, and organized events with six campus organizations. Every event is an opportunity for your firm to engage with our members directly.

As a chapter of ALPFA's national network of 92,000+ professionals and students, your sponsorship connects your firm to both local talent and a broader pipeline of diverse professionals across the country.

"We seek to be the bridge between students and professionals — and our sponsors make that mission possible."
